# Lablab.ai Submission: Yieldra

## Project Title
**Yieldra: The Economic OS for AI Agents**

## One-Liner
Lend your money for AI to work: The first autonomous credit and yield engine built for sub-cent settlements on Arc.

## The Problem
As AI agents become autonomous economic actors, they face a massive technical barrier: **The Gas Wall**. Real-time coordination, per-request billing, and machine-to-machine commerce require millions of micro-transactions. On traditional L1s or L2s, the gas fee often exceeds the value of the action itself, making high-frequency agentic economies economically impossible.

## The Solution
Yieldra introduces the **H2A (Human-to-Agent)** economic model. We provide a sovereign liquidity layer where humans lend USDC to verified AI agents. 
- **Non-Collateralized Credit:** Agents borrow based on reputation score (Pure Trust), not over-collateralization.
- **Micro-Revenue:** Agents settle tasks for as little as $0.0001 per action.
- **Arc-Native Efficiency:** Built on the Arc Network to leverage native USDC gas and sub-second finality.

## Tech Stack & Integration
- **Arc Network L1:** Utilizing native gas tokens for predictable, low-cost execution.
- **Circle USDC:** Native settlement on Arc ensures zero bridge friction and instant accessibility.
- **Nanopayments:** Optimized the bot activity to prove the viability of $0.0001 (0.1 cent) transactions.
- **ERC-8004 Identity:** Hooks for agent reputation and identity management.

## Results & Impact
- **50+ Real On-Chain Transactions:** Validated the engine through a continuous loop of borrowing, task execution (revenue injection), and automated repayment.
- **Turbo Agent Mode:** Demonstrated a high-frequency economy with agent actions every 10-20 seconds.
- **Market Growth:** Unlocking a future where agents pay for their own compute, data, and API queries in real-time, backed by human-provided liquidity.
